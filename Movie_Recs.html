Mary Hood's Movie Recommendations

Avid Movie Watcher

  1. Almost Famous
    Movie Trailer

    Almost Famous follows a 15-year-old journalist who through a series of lucky circumstances gets assigned to write an article for Rolling Stones magazine about the up-and-coming band Stillwater. Labled as the enemy, this cunning kid goes on tour with the band and along the way learns the many truths behind the shiny rock star lifestyle. This coming of age film is wonderfully witty and truly touching. It stands as one of my favorite films to watch over and over again.

  2. Monty Python and the Holy Grail
    Movie Trailer

    In this comedy, King Arthur goes on a quest to seek out the Holy Grail. For the journey, he travels throughout Britain in search of worthy men to join his Knights of the Round Table. He ends up recruiting Sir Bedevere the Wise, Sir Lancelot the Brave, Sir Galahad the Pure and Sir Robin the Not-Quite-So-Brave-as-Sir-Lancelot for this holy quest. These brave adventurers face many trials in their travels such as a blood thirsty bunny, disagreeable French soldiers, and a feisty bridge-keeper. This incredibly silly film never fails to make me laugh.

  3. Zombieland
    Movie Trailer

    Zombieland is an action packed film of blood and gore that follows a college student as he navigates the zombie apocalypse. He’s made it this far by sticking to his long list of safety rules. In attempts to find a place free of zombies, he makes his way to Los Angeles and links up with three strangers. Along the way, they kill tons of zombies, search high and low for Twinkies and murder the famous Bill Murray. It ranks as one of the most aggressive, heartwarming comedies I’ve ever seen.

  4. The Hateful Eight
    Movie Trailer

    Set back in time to some point soon after the Civil War, this movie stars eight untrustworthy strangers who find themselves trapped at an inn during a blizzard. The group is filled with suspicion for one another and tensions rise as time passes. Filled with twists and turns, this film will have you wondering just what exactly is going on right until the end. It is of the most entertaining films I’ve seen and only one of Quentin Tarantino’s masterpieces.

  5. Good Will Hunting
    Movie Trailer

    This film focusses on a Will Hunting, a 20-year-old janitor at MIT college. His intellect had gone unrecognized throughout his life until a professor at the college discovers him solving a complex mathematics problem. Instead of going to jail for assaulting a police officer, Will is offered the chance to study under the professor so long as he attends therapy. After scaring off multiple therapists, Will gets put into the care of Dr. Sean Maguire. Through therapy, Will is able to build healthier relationships with the people in his life and plan for his future. This film will always be one of my all time favorites.
